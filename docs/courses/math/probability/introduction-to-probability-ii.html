Introduction To Probability II

Category: Probability Published: Monday, 25 May 2015

This is a ungraduate/graduate level course being offered at Indiana University by the Math Department. I took this course on Fall 2012 with Prof. Russell Lyons. The textbook we used was An Introduction to Stochastic Modeling, Third Edition, by Taylor and Karlin, a rather old textbook but still quite useful. I would look for a new book and use this book as a complement rather than the main textbook for a course on Stochastic Processes (which is what this course is about).
